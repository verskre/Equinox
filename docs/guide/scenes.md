# Scenes

Equinox uses Derivative's Scene Changer to handle scenes. 

### Adding scenes

Navigate to the explorer on the bottom deck and select the 'Scenes' tab. 

Select 'Import scene .tox'. From the opened file explorer, select your scene. Your scene will be imported into the VJ.

### Deleting scenes

Navigate to the explorer and hover over the scene you wish to delete. Click on the 'X' button on the right side of the container.
